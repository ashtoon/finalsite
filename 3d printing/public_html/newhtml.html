Article Summary

In the past two years there has been tremendous advancements in the 3d printing industry, even catching the interest of Barack Obama when he stated "3d Printing is the wave of the future," Educational Institutes across the nation have recognized how their programs can benefit from incorporating 3d printing into their facilities.

3d printing has a vast potential. people from all over the world can share "blueprints" of designs they have made themselves. these Blueprints can range from model planes to gun components to artificial ears. the average 3d printer can cost anywhere from $30,000 to $100000, whereas the higher end ones can cost $400000.
